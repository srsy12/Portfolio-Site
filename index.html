About

Hi, my name is Stephen Sy and I love learning! I am no stranger to high pressure and mentally strenuous situations, allowing me to graduate from App Academy, coding for hours every day for the better part of a year whilst keeping a job at the same time. Throughout my coding journey, I have become proficient in many different popular technologies used in the industry. I am well versed in both JavaScript and Python and have used both languages to solve many Leetcode questions, as well as build full stack applications using other technologies such as: Express.js, Flask, Sequelize, SQLAlchemy, React.js with Redux, and AWS S3 just to name a few. I am not perfect by any means, but I can learn to get as close as I possibly can. Challenges do not intimidate me and instead motivate me by giving me a goal to aim for. I love playing games including League of Legends, and especially puzzle/logic games leading me to start taking chess a little bit more seriously. Feel free to reach out!
